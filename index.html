Tasty Ground Turkey Tacos

Tasty turkey tacos with a simple ingredients list to feed 6. The recipe is about 20 minutes of preperation and 35 minutes of cooking. It's a great time to spend time with your loved ones and enjoy these tasty tacos together!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs of ground turkey
  • 8 ounce can tomato sauce
  • 8 fl oz water
  • 4 oz can diced green chile peppers
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1.25 ounce package of taco seasoing mix
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 12(6 inch) corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro, or to taste
  • 2 tbps slices olives, or to taste

    • Steps

    1. Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add turkey; cook and stir until browned, about 5 minutes. Add tomato sauice, water, green chille peppers, onion, taco seasoning, and garlic; simmer, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 30 minutes

    2. Spoon turkey mixtures over tortillas. Top with lettuce, cheddar cheese, tomatoes, green onion, cilantro, and sliced olives. Again, add these ingredients to your taste or liking.
